About

A job portal web application is a platform that allows job seekers to search and apply for job openings, and for employers to post job openings and find suitable candidates. It typically includes features such as a job search engine, user profiles, job application tracking, resume builder, job alerts, and communication tools for job seekers and employers. The web app may also include additional features like skill assessments, interview scheduling, and salary comparison tools. The aim of a job portal web app is to connect job seekers and employers, and make the job search and hiring process more efficient and effective.
